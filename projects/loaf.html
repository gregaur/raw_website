Loaf
UX Flows, Branding, UI Designs

Loaf were in need of a new responsive website to showcase their delicious range of breads and pastry products.

Photography was a major factor in bringing their products to life on the website and we were relieved to hear they had a range of product shots already taken. We came up with a “Pinterest” style layout to showcase their wide range of pastry products as we wanted these to be visible and accessible right from the get go. Loaf had very little in the way of branding and basically all I had to work with was the product packaging and some brand colours. My role in this project included constant communication with the client.
